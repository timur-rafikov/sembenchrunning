--- TYPE HIERARCHY ---

  Everything that is a physical object is also a object
  Everything that is a medical device asset or a maintenance timeslot or a maintenance procedure or a staging area or a consumable kit or a auxiliary device or a service station or a external contractor token or a regulatory document or a certification record or a spare component record or a service report or a asset attribute is also a physical object
  Everything that is a device class or a vendor configuration is also a asset attribute
  Everything that is a portable device or a fixed device is also a medical device asset

--- ACTIONS ---

  create a maintenance case for a medical device asset A
    I can only create a maintenance case for a medical device asset A if it is not the case that the maintenance case for the device A is open and it is not the case that the case for A is closed
    Once I create a maintenance case for a medical device asset A, it is the case that the maintenance case for the device A is open

  allocate a maintenance timeslot B to a medical device asset A
    I can only allocate a maintenance timeslot B to a medical device asset A if it is the case that the maintenance case for the device A is open and B is available and A is compatible with B
    I can only allocate a maintenance timeslot B to a medical device asset A if it is not the case that A has a timeslot
    Once I allocate a maintenance timeslot B to a medical device asset A, it is the case that A is allocated to the timeslot B and A has a timeslot
    Once I allocate a maintenance timeslot B to a medical device asset A, it is not the case anymore that B is available

  release a maintenance timeslot B from a medical device asset A
    I can only release a maintenance timeslot B from a medical device asset A if it is the case that A is allocated to the timeslot B
    I can only release a maintenance timeslot B from a medical device asset A if it is not the case that the procedure for A has started and it is not the case that the verification for A is complete
    Once I release a maintenance timeslot B from a medical device asset A, it is the case that B is available
    Once I release a maintenance timeslot B from a medical device asset A, it is not the case anymore that A is allocated to the timeslot B and it is not the case anymore that A has a timeslot and it is not the case anymore that A is ready at the station and it is not the case anymore that preparation of A is complete and it is not the case anymore that A is on regulatory hold and it is not the case anymore that A is ready for finalization and it is not the case anymore that A requires additional inspection

  reserve a service station B for a medical device asset A
