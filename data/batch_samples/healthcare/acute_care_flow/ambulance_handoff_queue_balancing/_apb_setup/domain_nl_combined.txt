  Everything that is a physical object is also a object
  Everything that is a patient episode or a handoff bay or a treatment area or a nurse resource or a physician resource or a special equipment or a stretcher gurney or a paramedic partner or a consultation slot or a triage room or a diagnostic slot or a bed type or a clinical team is also a physical object
