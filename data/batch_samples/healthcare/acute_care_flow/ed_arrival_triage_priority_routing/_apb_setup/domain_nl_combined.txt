--- TYPE HIERARCHY ---

  Everything that is a entity is also a object
  Everything that is a patient or a triage staff or a care area or a diagnostic service or a clinician or a specialty service or a stretcher or a registration kiosk or a treatment room or a porter or a imaging equipment or a consultant or a bed is also a entity
  Everything that is a monitored bed or a observation bed is also a bed
  Everything that is a adult patient or a pediatric patient is also a patient

--- ACTIONS ---

  register the arrival of a patient A
    I can only register the arrival of a patient A if it is not the case that A is registered as a patient and it is not the case that the disposition is finalized for A
    Once I register the arrival of a patient A, it is the case that A is registered as a patient

  assign a triage staff B to a patient A for triage
    I can only assign a triage staff B to a patient A for triage if it is the case that A is registered as a patient and B is available for triage and B is compatible with A for triage
    I can only assign a triage staff B to a patient A for triage if it is not the case that triage is in progress for A
    Once I assign a triage staff B to a patient A for triage, it is the case that A is assigned to triage staff B and triage is in progress for A
    Once I assign a triage staff B to a patient A for triage, it is not the case anymore that B is available for triage

  unassign a triage staff B from a patient A after triage completion
    I can only unassign a triage staff B from a patient A after triage completion if it is the case that A is assigned to triage staff B
    I can only unassign a triage staff B from a patient A after triage completion if it is not the case that A is in treatment and it is not the case that safety checks are completed for A
    Once I unassign a triage staff B from a patient A after triage completion, it is the case that B is available for triage
    Once I unassign a triage staff B from a patient A after triage completion, it is not the case anymore that A is assigned to triage staff B and it is not the case anymore that triage is in progress for A and it is not the case anymore that triage assessment is complete for A and it is not the case anymore that A is ready for care assignment and it is not the case anymore that A has a priority flag and it is not the case anymore that A is ready for bed assignment and it is not the case anymore that escalation is required for A

  allocate a stretcher B to a patient A

    Once I allocate a stretcher B to a patient A, it is the case that A is assigned to B
    Once I allocate a stretcher B to a patient A, it is not the case anymore that B is available
